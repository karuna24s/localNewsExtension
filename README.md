## Local News Extension

### Introduction

Google Chrome extension where users can look up articles of political candidates from local newspapers in Pennsylvania such as:
  * Times-Tribune
  * Daily Local
  * Citizen's Voice
  * Standard Speaker
  * Times Leader
  * Wayne Independent
  * Pocono Record
  * Pike County Courier
  * Chester County Press
  * Twitter Alerts
  * Facebook Alerts
